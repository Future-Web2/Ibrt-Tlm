Our Story & Mission

Founded by young entrepreneur Shahriyor Hamrayev, IBRAT Talim started its journey from a single school classroom and has rapidly grown into a widespread network of educational branches. Our mission is clear: to provide modern, high-quality education created by the youth, for the youth.

Modern Methods

We utilize the latest educational techniques including Montessori methods and interactive learning environments to foster rapid growth.

Expert Teachers

Our educators are highly qualified professionals dedicated to creating engaging and effective learning experiences for all age groups.
